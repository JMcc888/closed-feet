About us

Who We Are:

In 2007 James Holmes decided to open his own healthcare agency because of his passion he has for people and wanting to give back to the community. Open Hands Nursing, LLC in home health care is designed to meet the unique needs of clients and their families. We provide flexibility, creative problem solving, and open communication to clients and their families. Our goal is to empower the population we serve in the care of Open Hands Nursing Agency, llc in-home health care.

Mission Statement

To serve our clients with dignity, respect and compassion by coming together as one body to insure that clients and their families receive comprehensive, professional service and quality care.
